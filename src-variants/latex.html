We present a minimal personal homepage of one Gregor Grigorjan, comprising contact information and a brief biographical statement. The document is served as static HTML and CSS, free of JavaScript, web fonts, images, third-party trackers, and CDN dependencies. We argue informally that this approach yields favourable transmission characteristics.
