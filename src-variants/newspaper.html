Local Engineer Continues Tinkering With Emacs, Sources Confirm

Open-source contributor and NixOS enthusiast publishes a website containing exactly zero (0) JavaScript. Exclusive

Tallinn — In a development that is unlikely to surprise anyone familiar with the suspect's habits, Gregor Grigorjan has once again been observed writing software in functional languages, witnesses confirmed late Monday.

Mr. Grigorjan, who declined to comment beyond a brief reply written entirely inside Emacs, is said to value reproducible systems, sharp tools, and the keeping of things simple. Acquaintances describe him as “the kind of person who runs NixOS on purpose.”

The website on which this article appears ships no JavaScript, no web fonts, no images, no trackers, and no requests to any content delivery network — a fact local readers may or may not find newsworthy.
